Red Hot Chilli Pepers

The Californian funk-rock crew reunite with guitarist John Frusciante (again) for an all-out, jam-band journey. - Neil Z. Yeung

Confidence Man

Euphoric surge of '90s house-indebted dance anthems from this party-loving Australian quartet. - Neil Z. Yeung

North Mississippi Allstars

The Hill Siblings wed sultry Southern funk to soul, blues, and psychedelia on their most adventurous outing yet. - Thom Jurek

Papercuts - Past Life Regression

Almost the equal of the band's career-defining Parallel Universe Blues, this is droning, melancholy, VU-influenced indie pop at its finest. - Tim Sendra

Avatars of Love

A constantly surprising, alternately epic and intimate album from the songwriter that feels like a definitive artistic statement. - Tim Sendra

Meshuggah - Immutable

After six long years, Sweden's metal innovators indulge their trademark sound while expanding its reach in several directions. - Thom Jurek
